Penne Rigate n.73 is a classic ridged short-cut dry pasta produced by bronze-die extrusion and slow drying. The ridged surface and tubular shape make it suitable for both smooth and chunky sauces, baked dishes, and cold pasta salads.

## 2. Ingredients

**Ingredients:** durum wheat semolina, water.

No additives, preservatives, colourings, or eggs are used in the formulation.

## 3. Allergens

In accordance with Regulation (EU) No 1169/2011 (Annex II), the following allergens are present and are highlighted in **bold** in the ingredient list on pack.

**Allergens present:**

- gluten

**May contain (cross-contact traces):**

- soy

The plant processes cereals containing gluten and soy on shared lines; the "may contain" statement reflects the unavoidable risk of cross-contact despite allergen management controls.
