## 3. Allergen declaration

### Allergens present
The following allergens are present in the product and are highlighted in the ingredient list on the consumer pack, in compliance with Reg. (EU) 1169/2011 Annex II:

- **gluten**

### "May contain" traces
Cross-contamination statement based on the plant allergen risk assessment:

- The product carries **no "may contain" traces**. There are no declared cross-contamination allergens for this SKU.

> Editorial note: an earlier revision of this sheet listed a possible **soy** trace for this SKU. That entry was a documentation error and has been **superseded** - PAS-FUS-500 carries no "may contain" traces.

> Note: Al Dente S.r.l. operates an egg-free plant. Other SKUs in the catalogue may carry "may contain" statements for soy and/or mustard; this SKU (PAS-FUS-500) does not.
