## 2. Composition

### 2.1 Ingredients

durum wheat semolina, water

The product contains no eggs, no colourings, no preservatives and no additives. It is produced from durum wheat semolina (semola di grano duro) and water only, following the standard bronze-die / controlled drying production cycle for Al Dente dry pasta.

### 2.2 Allergens

The following allergens are **present** in the product (declared in bold on the consumer label, per Reg. (EU) 1169/2011):

- **gluten**

### 2.3 "May contain" traces

Due to shared lines and the manufacturing environment, the product may contain traces of:

- soy
- mustard

These cross-contact statements ("may contain: soy, mustard") are precautionary and reflect the plant's allergen risk assessment. The Al Dente plant is an egg-free facility.
