## 2. Composition

**Ingredients:** durum wheat semolina, water

The product is made exclusively from durum wheat semolina and water. No colourings, preservatives, eggs or additives are used.

## 3. Allergen information

**Contains:** gluten

**May contain (traces):** soy

The product contains gluten (from durum wheat). Due to shared production lines and the supply chain, the product may contain traces of soy. No other allergens are intentionally added.

| Allergen | Present | May contain (traces) |
|----------|:-------:|:--------------------:|
| Gluten (durum wheat) | Yes | - |
| Soy | - | Yes |
