## 2. Composition

### Ingredients

durum wheat semolina, water

No additives, no preservatives, no eggs are used in this product.

### Allergens

The following allergens are present in the product:

- **gluten**

### "May contain" traces

Due to shared lines and the plant environment, the product may contain traces of:

- **soy**
- **mustard**

These traces are declared on the retail packaging in accordance with allergen labelling requirements.

## 3. Shelf life

| Field | Value |

| Shelf life | 36 months |

The best-before date (TMC) is printed on each retail box and on the carton. Shelf life is guaranteed when the product is stored in a cool, dry place, away from direct sunlight and strong odours, in its original sealed packaging.
