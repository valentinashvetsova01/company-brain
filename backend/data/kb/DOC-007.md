The Tagliatele n.201 is a long, ribbon-cut dry durum wheat pasta, format reference n.201, sold in a 500g cardboard box for retail and food-service distribution.

---

## 2. Ingredients

**Ingredients:** durum wheat semolina, water

The product is made exclusively from durum wheat semolina (semola di grano duro) and water. No additives, colourings, preservatives or eggs are used.

---

## 3. Allergens

In accordance with Regulation (EU) No 1169/2011, the following allergens are present and declared in **bold** on the label:

- **Gluten**

### "May contain" traces

Due to shared production and packaging lines, the product may contain traces of:

- soy
- mustard

These traces are stated as a precautionary allergen labelling statement ("may contain").
