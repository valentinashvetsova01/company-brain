## 2. Description

Conchiglie n.51 is a short-cut dry pasta produced from durum wheat semolina through bronze-die extrusion and slow drying, in line with Al Dente S.r.l. production standards. Each consumer unit is a 500g box.

## 3. Ingredients

durum wheat semolina, water

## 4. Allergen information

**Allergens (present):**

- gluten

**May contain (cross-contact traces):**

- soy
- mustard

Allergen declaration is stated in compliance with EU Reg. 1169/2011. The plant operates with lot-level traceability; the "may contain" statement reflects possible cross-contact on shared lines.

## 5. Shelf life

- **Shelf life:** 36 months from the production date, when stored in a cool, dry place away from direct sunlight and strong odours.
- The "best before" date (TMC) is printed on each consumer unit and on the carton.
