This specification refers to the dry pasta shape "Orecchiette", bronze-die drawn and slow-dried, packed in a 500g retail box.

## 2. Ingredients

**Ingredients:** durum wheat semolina, water.

The product is made exclusively from durum wheat semolina and water, with no added eggs, colourings, or preservatives.

## 3. Allergens

In accordance with Regulation (EU) No 1169/2011, the following allergens are present and declared in bold on the label:

- **gluten**

### "May contain" traces

Due to shared lines and the manufacturing environment, the product may contain traces of:

- soy
- mustard

## 4. Shelf life

| Field | Value |
|-------|-------|
| Shelf life | 36 months |

Best before end (TMC) is printed on each retail unit and on the carton. Shelf life of **36 months** applies to product stored in a cool, dry place, away from direct sunlight and strong odours.

## 7. Storage and handling

Store in a cool, dry place. Once opened, reseal the pack and consume within the indicated shelf life. Avoid exposure to humidity, heat sources, and direct sunlight to preserve cooking quality.
