## 1. Product Identification

| Field | Value |
|-------|-------|
| Product name | Bucatini n.9 - 500g box |
| SKU | PAS-BUC-500 |

| Format | 500g |

Bucatini n.9 is a long, hollow-tube dry pasta produced exclusively from selected durum wheat semolina. It is intended for retail (GDO) and food-service distribution channels and is supplied in the standard 500g consumer box.

---

## 2. Ingredients

**Ingredients:** durum wheat semolina, water.

No additives, preservatives, colourings or flavourings are used. The product contains no egg and no animal-derived ingredients.

---

## 3. Allergens

In accordance with Regulation (EU) No 1169/2011, the following allergens are declared.

**Contains:**

- gluten

**May contain (cross-contact traces):**

- soy
- mustard

The "may contain" statement reflects shared production and packaging lines and is applied on a precautionary basis. The plant is egg-free.

## 4. Shelf Life

| Field | Value |
|-------|-------|
| Shelf life (TMC / best before) | 36 months |

The best-before date (TMC) is printed on each consumer unit and on the carton. Shelf life is guaranteed when the product is stored in a cool, dry place, away from direct sunlight and strong odours, in its original sealed packaging.

---

## 7. Storage and Handling

- Store in a cool, dry place below 25 C, relative humidity below 65%.
- Keep packaging sealed and intact until use.
- Do not stack pallets beyond the limits indicated on the carton.
- Protect from direct sunlight, heat sources and strong odours.

---

## 8. Cooking Instructions

Bring abundant salted water to a rolling boil, add the pasta and cook for the time indicated on the box, stirring occasionally. Drain and season to taste.
