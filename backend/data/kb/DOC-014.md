# GDO Supply Specification (Capitolato)

**Document ID:** DOC-014
**Document type:** GDO supply specification (capitolato di fornitura)
**Company:** Al Dente S.r.l. - Dry pasta manufacturing
**Status:** Approved and in force
**Owner:** Sales & Logistics Department
**Review cycle:** Annual, or upon change of the customer's supply framework

---

## 1. Purpose

This supply specification (capitolato) defines the mandatory requirements that Al Dente S.r.l. applies to the supply of dry pasta to Large-Scale Retail (GDO - Grande Distribuzione Organizzata) customers. It sets out the labeling, palletization, delivery and documentation standards that govern every order accepted under a GDO supply agreement.

The requirements in this document are binding. An order is considered correctly fulfilled only when all the conditions described below are met. Where a GDO customer's own framework imposes stricter requirements, the stricter requirement prevails, but it may never fall below the minimum standards stated here.

## 2. Scope

This specification applies to all finished dry pasta products supplied by Al Dente S.r.l. to GDO channels, across all distribution centres (CeDi) and points of delivery designated by the customer. It covers the product as it is presented for shipment: the saleable unit, the carton (collo), the pallet and the accompanying transport documentation.

## 3. Labeling

**Labeling: EAN-13 per unit + carton.**

Every saleable unit and every carton must carry an EAN-13 barcode.

- **Per unit:** each retail-ready saleable unit bears its own EAN-13 code, enabling scanning at the point of sale.
- **Per carton (collo):** each carton bears an EAN-13 code identifying the packed product, enabling scanning and goods-in reconciliation at the customer's distribution centre.

Both barcodes must be present, legible and scannable on first read. The unit-level EAN-13 and the carton-level EAN-13 are distinct codes serving distinct purposes; neither may be omitted. Labels must be applied so that they remain intact and readable through handling, transport and storage.

## 4. Palletization

**Pallet: EUR pallet, 80x120.**

All deliveries are palletized on a EUR pallet (Euro-pallet) with a footprint of 80x120 cm. Cartons are arranged in stable, regular layers within the pallet footprint, with no overhang beyond the pallet edges, so that the load can be handled, stacked and stored safely throughout the supply chain.

Each pallet is built to present its cartons consistently and to allow the EAN-13 carton labels (Section 3) to be scanned during goods-in operations.

## 5. Delivery

**Delivery window: 48-72h.**

Deliveries are made within a delivery window of 48-72h from order confirmation, to the point of delivery indicated by the GDO customer. The 48-72h window is the committed lead time for standard GDO orders under this specification.

Al Dente S.r.l. plans production and dispatch so that the agreed delivery window is respected. Any condition that could prevent delivery within 48-72h is communicated to the customer in advance.

## 6. Transport Documentation

**DDT required: yes.**

A Documento di Trasporto (DDT - transport document) is mandatory and must accompany every delivery. No shipment is dispatched, and no delivery is accepted, without the corresponding DDT.

The DDT identifies the goods being transported and is the reference document for goods-in reconciliation at the customer's site. It is matched against the physical delivery - units, cartons and pallets - and against the EAN-13 labeling, to confirm that what is delivered corresponds to what was ordered.

## 7. Summary of Requirements

| Requirement | Standard |
|-------------|----------|
| **Labeling** | EAN-13 per unit + carton |
| **Pallet** | EUR pallet, 80x120 |
| **Delivery window** | 48-72h |
| **DDT** | Required |

All four requirements are mandatory. A delivery that fails to meet any one of them is non-conforming with this specification and is subject to rejection or to corrective action agreed with the customer.

## 8. Responsibilities

- The **Sales & Logistics Department** owns this specification and ensures that every accepted GDO order is planned and fulfilled in line with the requirements above.
- **Production & Packaging** ensures that unit-level and carton-level EAN-13 labels are correctly applied before palletization.
- **Warehouse & Dispatch** ensures correct EUR pallet (80x120) palletization, dispatch within the 48-72h delivery window, and issuance of the DDT accompanying each delivery.
