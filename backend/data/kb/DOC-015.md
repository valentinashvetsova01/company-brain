## 1. Scope and conditions

This price list sets out the official **wholesale list prices** for Al Dente S.r.l. dry pasta products for the 2026 commercial year. Prices are expressed in euro (EUR) per carton of 20 retail units (500g boxes), excluding VAT.

- All products are supplied in cartons of 20 retail units unless otherwise agreed.
- List prices are gross of any commercial discount, volume rebate, or promotional condition. Customer-specific net conditions are defined in the relevant supply agreement.
- Prices are quoted ex works (EXW) Al Dente S.r.l. plant, unless different delivery terms are stated in the framework contract.
- Labelling and palletisation follow the GDO supply specification (see DOC-014).
