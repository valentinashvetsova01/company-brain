## 1. Product identification

| Field | Value |
|---|---|
| Commercial name | Spaghetti n.5 Bio - 500g box |
| Internal SKU | PAS-SPA-BIO-500 |
| Product category | Dry semolina pasta (long cut), organic |
| Format | 500g |
| EAN code | 80191967779 |

Spaghetti n.5 Bio is the certified organic counterpart of our classic long-cut spaghetti. It shares the same die geometry and slow-drying profile as the conventional line, while sourcing exclusively organically farmed durum wheat.

## 2. Composition

**Ingredients:** organic durum wheat semolina, water

The product is manufactured exclusively from organic durum wheat semolina and water. No additives, preservatives, colourings or eggs are used. All semolina lots are accompanied by valid organic certification documents from approved suppliers and are verified at goods-in before release to production.

## 3. Allergen information

**Contains:** gluten

**May contain (traces):** soy

Allergen declaration is provided in accordance with Regulation (EU) No 1169/2011. Allergens present in the product are highlighted in bold on the consumer label. The "may contain" statement reflects cross-contamination risk from shared lines and the production environment, and is reviewed whenever the production layout or the ingredient portfolio changes.

## 4. Shelf life

| Field | Value |
|---|---|
| Shelf life (TMC / best before) | 36 months |

The minimum durability date (best before / TMC) is set at 36 months from the production date, when stored under the conditions below.

**Storage conditions:** store in a cool, dry place, away from direct sunlight and strong odours. Once the box is opened, the product should be kept in a closed container and consumed within a reasonable time.
