## 1. Product identification

| Field | Value |

| Internal SKU | PAS-PEN-BIO-500 |

Penne Rigate n.73 Bio is a short, ridged cut belonging to the Al Dente organic line. The ridged surface is designed to hold sauces, making the format suitable for both household and food-service preparations.

## 2. Composition

**Ingredients:** organic durum wheat semolina, water

The product is manufactured exclusively from organic durum wheat semolina and water. The semolina originates from certified organic farming. No additives, preservatives, colourings or eggs are used. The organic status is maintained throughout the supply chain and is verified under the applicable organic certification scheme.

## 3. Allergen information

**Contains:** gluten

**May contain (traces):** None declared

Allergen declaration is provided in accordance with Regulation (EU) No 1169/2011. The product contains gluten, which is highlighted in bold on the consumer label. No additional cross-contamination allergens are declared for this product.
