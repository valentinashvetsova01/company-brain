## 1. Product identification

| Field | Value |
|---|---|
| Commercial name | Rigatoni n.24 Bio - 500g box |
| Internal SKU | PAS-RIG-BIO-500 |
| Product category | Organic dry semolina pasta (short cut, ridged) |

| EAN code | 80855637271 |

Rigatoni n.24 Bio is a short, ridged tubular pasta produced from certified organic raw materials. The ridged surface and wide tube are designed to hold rich, structured sauces, making the product suitable for both retail and food-service channels.

## 2. Composition

**Ingredients:** organic durum wheat semolina, water

The product is manufactured exclusively from organic durum wheat semolina and water. No additives, preservatives, colourings or eggs are used. The durum wheat semolina is sourced from certified organic supply chains, and organic status is maintained throughout milling, dough preparation, extrusion and drying.

## 3. Allergen information

**Contains:** gluten

**May contain (traces):** soy

Allergen declaration is provided in accordance with Regulation (EU) No 1169/2011. Allergens present in the product are highlighted in bold on the consumer label. The "may contain" statement reflects cross-contamination risk from shared lines and the production environment.

## 4. Shelf life

| Field | Value |

The minimum durability date (best before / TMC) is set at 36 months from the production date, when stored under the conditions below.
