The Linguine n.13 Bio is part of the Al Dente organic range, produced from certified organic raw materials and bronze-drawn to obtain a rough surface that holds sauces well.

## 2. Composition

**Ingredients:** organic durum wheat semolina, water

The product is manufactured exclusively from organic durum wheat semolina and water. No additives, preservatives, colourings or eggs are used. The durum wheat semolina is sourced from certified organic supply chains.

## 3. Allergen information

**Contains:** gluten

**May contain (traces):** soy, mustard

Allergen declaration is provided in accordance with Regulation (EU) No 1169/2011. Allergens present in the product are highlighted in bold on the consumer label. The "may contain" statement reflects cross-contamination risk from shared lines and the production environment.
