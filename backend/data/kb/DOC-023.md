## 1. Product identification

| Field | Value |
|---|---|
| Commercial name | Spaghetti n.5 - 250g box |
| Internal SKU | PAS-SPA-250 |
| Product category | Dry semolina pasta (long cut) |

This data sheet describes the 250g consumer format of Spaghetti n.5. It shares the same recipe and process as the other formats in the Spaghetti n.5 range and differs only in the consumer unit size and related logistics parameters.

## 2. Composition

**Ingredients:** durum wheat semolina, water

The product is manufactured exclusively from durum wheat semolina and water. No additives, preservatives, colourings or eggs are used. The semolina is sourced and controlled to ensure consistent protein content and milling quality, supporting a firm cooking behaviour typical of the Spaghetti n.5 cut.

## 3. Allergen information

**Contains:** gluten

**May contain (traces):** None declared

Allergen declaration is provided in accordance with Regulation (EU) No 1169/2011. Allergens present in the product are highlighted in bold on the consumer label. Gluten is inherent to the durum wheat semolina used as the base raw material. No additional cross-contamination traces are declared for this product on the basis of the current line and raw-material risk assessment.

## 4. Shelf life

| Field | Value |
|---|---|
| Shelf life (TMC / best before) | 36 months |

The minimum durability date (best before / TMC) is set at 36 months from the production date, when stored under the conditions below. The long shelf life reflects the low moisture content of dry semolina pasta and the protective primary packaging.

**Storage conditions:** store in a cool, dry place, away from direct sunlight and strong odours. Once opened, keep the remaining product sealed and protected from humidity.

## 5. Nutritional values (per 100g)

| Nutrient | Per 100g |
|---|---|
| Energy | 351 kcal |
| Protein | 12.2 g |
| Carbohydrate | 70.1 g |

Values are average figures referred to the product as sold (dry, uncooked). Cooking in water without added fat does not materially alter the relative macronutrient profile, though the absolute values per portion depend on the cooked weight.
