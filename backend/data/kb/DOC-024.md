## 1. Product identification

| Internal SKU | PAS-PEN-250 |
| Product category | Dry semolina pasta (short cut, ridged) |

Penne Rigate n.73 is a short-cut dry pasta with a ridged (rigate) surface, designed to hold sauces well. It is supplied in a 250g consumer box for retail and food-service channels.

## 2. Composition

**Ingredients:** durum wheat semolina, water

The product is manufactured exclusively from durum wheat semolina and water. No additives, preservatives, colourings or eggs are used. The bronze-style die and controlled drying give the ridged surface its characteristic texture.

## 3. Allergen information

**Contains:** gluten

**May contain (traces):** soy, mustard

Allergen declaration is provided in accordance with Regulation (EU) No 1169/2011. Allergens present in the product are highlighted in bold on the consumer label. The "may contain" statement reflects cross-contamination risk from shared lines and the production environment.
