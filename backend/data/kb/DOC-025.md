## 1. Product identification

| Field | Value |
|---|---|
| Commercial name | Fusilli n.98 - 250g box |
| Internal SKU | PAS-FUS-250 |
| Product category | Dry semolina pasta (short cut, spiral shape) |
| Format | 250g |
| EAN code | 80125234507 |

The Fusilli n.98 is a classic short spiral-cut dry pasta produced for the 250g consumer box. The helical shape is designed to hold sauces well and is suited to both cold and warm preparations.

## 2. Composition

**Ingredients:** durum wheat semolina, water

The product is manufactured exclusively from durum wheat semolina and water. No additives, preservatives, colourings or eggs are used. The dough is bronze-influenced extruded and slow dried to preserve the texture of the durum wheat semolina.

## 3. Allergen information

**Contains:** gluten

**May contain (traces):** None declared

Allergen declaration is provided in accordance with Regulation (EU) No 1169/2011. Allergens present in the product are highlighted in bold on the consumer label. For this SKU no cross-contamination traces are declared on the label; the only allergen present is gluten, intrinsic to the durum wheat semolina.

## 4. Shelf life

| Field | Value |
|---|---|
| Shelf life (TMC / best before) | 36 months |

The minimum durability date (best before / TMC) is set at 36 months from the production date, when stored under the conditions below.

**Storage conditions:** store in a cool, dry place, away from direct sunlight and strong odours. Once opened, keep the box sealed and consume within a reasonable period to preserve quality.
