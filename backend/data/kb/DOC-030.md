# Logistics service level - national deliveries

**Document ID:** DOC-030
**Issued by:** Al Dente S.r.l. - Logistics Department
**Scope:** Outbound deliveries of finished product to customers within the national territory
**Applies to:** Logistics operators, Shipping clerks, Warehouse supervisor, Customer service
**Related documents:** GDO supply specification (DOC-014), Returns and quality complaints policy (DOC-011), Quality management policy (DOC-012), HACCP plan (DOC-013), Incoming goods QC procedure (DOC-016), Returns handling procedure (DOC-017)

---

## 1. Purpose

This document defines the logistics service level that Al Dente S.r.l. commits to for national deliveries of finished pasta to its customers. It sets out the expected delivery timing, the handling unit used for shipments, the service performance target against which logistics is measured, and how transport damage is reported at the point of delivery.

The objective is to give a single, consistent reference for how orders are dispatched and delivered, so that both the Logistics Department and the customer have a shared understanding of what "on time and in full" means and how exceptions are managed.

---

## 2. Scope and applicability

This service level applies to all standard outbound deliveries of finished product shipped from the Al Dente S.r.l. plant to destinations within the national territory. It covers the movement of goods from the moment an order is released for shipping to the moment it is received and signed for by the customer.

For deliveries to large-scale retail customers, the terms of this document operate alongside the GDO supply specification (DOC-014); where the customer specification sets stricter requirements, those requirements take precedence. Returns arising after delivery are handled separately under the returns and quality complaints policy (DOC-011) and the returns handling procedure (DOC-017).

---

## 3. Service level commitments

### 3.1 Delivery window

Standard national orders are delivered within a **delivery window of 48-72 hours** from order release. This window is measured from the point at which the order is confirmed and released for shipping, to the point of delivery at the customer's designated address.

The 48-72 hour window assumes a complete and confirmed order with no holds on the customer account and a deliverable destination within the national territory.

### 3.2 Handling unit

Finished product is shipped on the standard **EUR pallet (80x120)**. Goods are palletised, stacked and stabilised according to the load plan so that they travel safely and arrive intact. Using a single standard handling unit keeps loading, transport and goods-in at the customer consistent and predictable.

### 3.3 Service performance target

Logistics performance for national deliveries is measured against an **on-time-in-full (OTIF) target of 95 percent**. An order counts as OTIF only when it is delivered both within the 48-72 hour delivery window and complete, with no missing or short-supplied lines.

OTIF is monitored on a continuous basis. Sustained performance below the 95 percent target triggers a review by the Logistics Department to identify and correct the root cause.

---

## 4. Transport damage at delivery

If a delivery arrives with visible transport damage, the receiving party must **annotate the damage on the DDT (delivery note) at the time of delivery**. The annotation on the DDT is the formal record that the goods were received in a damaged condition and is the starting point for any subsequent claim.

Damage noted on the DDT is reported to Al Dente S.r.l. customer service so that the case can be assessed. Where the issue concerns product conformity or quality rather than transport, it is routed into the returns and quality complaints policy (DOC-011) and handled under the returns handling procedure (DOC-017).

The table below summarises the logistics service parameters for national deliveries.

| Parameter | Commitment |
| --- | --- |
| Delivery window | 48-72 hours from order release |
| Handling unit | EUR pallet, 80x120 |
| OTIF target | 95 percent |
| Transport damage | Annotate on the DDT at delivery |

---

## 5. Responsibilities

- **Shipping clerk:** confirms the order is complete and released, prepares shipping documents including the DDT, and books transport so the 48-72 hour window can be met.
- **Logistics operator:** palletises goods on the EUR pallet (80x120), secures the load, and dispatches according to the load plan.
- **Warehouse supervisor:** ensures only complete, released orders are shipped and that handling units conform to the standard.
- **Customer service:** monitors OTIF against the 95 percent target, manages exceptions, and processes DDT-noted transport damage in coordination with the relevant policies.

---

This document is part of the Al Dente S.r.l. operating standards. Any deviation from the committed service level must be authorised by the Logistics Department and recorded.
